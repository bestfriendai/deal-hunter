# Deal Hunter

Your wallet's best friend. Always watching. Always saving.

## What It Does

Deal Hunter automatically tracks prices across 500+ stores and alerts you the second anything on your wishlist goes on sale. Never pay full price again.

## Features

- **Price Tracking** - Monitors 500+ stores automatically. Get alerts the SECOND prices drop.
- **Cash Back** - Earn money back on every purchase. Up to 40% on select items.
- **Instant Alerts** - Push notifications the moment your wishlist items go on sale.
